The Language of Scent

Discover
the Notes

Every Mega Frangrance Center fragrance is composed in three movements — an opening, a heart, and a lasting memory.

Top Notes

The first breath — bright, fleeting, and immediately captivating. Citrus, light woods, and fresh aromatics.

Heart Notes

The soul of the fragrance. Florals, spices, and resins that reveal themselves after the opening fades.

Base Notes

The lasting impression — rich musks, precious woods, and resins that remain hours after application.

Our Promise

Why Choose Mega Frangrance Center

01

Rare Ingredients

We source only the finest raw materials — ethically obtained oud from Assam, Bulgarian rose absolute, and Indonesian vetiver — to create compositions of genuine depth.

02

Unisex Philosophy

Fragrance has no gender. Every Mega Frangrance Center scent is designed to belong to whoever wears it — a meeting of presence and character, not expectation.

03

Small-Batch Craft

Each fragrance is produced in limited quantities by our in-house perfumers. No shortcuts. No mass-market compromises. Just exceptional, artisanal quality.

Our Story

Scent as a
State of Being

"We didn't set out to make perfume. We set out to capture something that words cannot."

Founded in New York City in 2019 by a collective of perfumers, artists, and dreamers, Mega Frangrance Center began as a simple question: what does presence smell like?

Four years and hundreds of formulations later, we have built a house that exists in the space between art and science — between luxury and accessibility.
